Playable Black Jack built on vanilla JS and deckofcardsapi.com

Instructions:

The total value of cards closest to 21 without going over (bust) wins

Aces are worth eleven, unless they would cause bust, then they are worth one

Hit to get another card, stand for dealers turn

Dealer must stand if their score is over 17 and must hit once if score is below 17
